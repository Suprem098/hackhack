Who Is OURBCA?

OURBCA is a Web and Mobile application that provides a complete set of reference materials like notes, syllabus, question banks, solutions, and much more for BCA students. We built this platform to provide the best possible resources for students so that they can excel in their academic journey.

Notes
It is often difficult to get complete notes of all subjects. That's where we come in and provide you with handwritten or typed notes.

Solutions
We also help you excel by providing the best possible solutions to every question of past papers or important questions in general.
